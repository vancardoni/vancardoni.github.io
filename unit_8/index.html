Our Impact

100% sustainable swimwear made from ocean recovered fishing nets and other recycled materials. Our swimwear label stand for postive change and empowerment. At Mare we believe in supporting the oceans in which our swimwear is made to swim. Made from only the best ECONYL regenerated nylon, we strive to run an eco-friendly business, office, shipping and customer experience. A percent of every piece manufactured & sold goes straight back to keeping our beaches clean and waste free.
